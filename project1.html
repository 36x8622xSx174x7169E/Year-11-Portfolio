Implications

Asthetics
Asthetics is about making sure the character looks good, this gives quality to our final product and is more appealing to the end users. I will do this by properly cloring and textiring my character and also make sure that my lighting and also the general shape of my character is correct.

Functionality
Functionality is what makes the end product work this means that the character is animated and is how it is supposed to look. I will do this by making sure my character dosent have any material clipping and isn't overdetailed. I will also make sure that my character is animated correctly and that the animation dosent break, i will achieve this by running the animation muiltiple times before saving.
Usability
Usability makes sure that the end product is easy animated correctly and is appealing to use. I will do this by making sure that i have a smooth animation that looks natural and dosent feel still or forced. I will make sure there is no clipping and responds correctly when an imput is recieved.
